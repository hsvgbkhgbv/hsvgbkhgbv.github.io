My name is Jianhong Wang and I graduated from University College London for MRes degree in Web Science and Big Data Analytics, supervised by Prof. Jun Wang and Imperial College London for MSc degree in Machine Learning, supervised by Prof. Bjoern Schuller. Now, I am pursuing my PhD degree at the department of Eletrical and Electronic Engineering in Imperial College London, supervised by Dr. Tae-Kyun Kim and Dr. Yunjie Gu. My current research interest is on Reinforcement Learning, Multi-agent Learning and Dialogue System. Finally, I am now partially sponsored by EPSRC and looking for some additional funding.
